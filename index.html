Fdf

This projects aims to create infographically a schematic representation of an embossed map by linking three different points (x, y, z) with straight lines. The coordinates of the map are stocked into a file just like this one:

- the horizontal position corresponds to the abscissa
- the vertical position corresponds to its ordinate
- the value corresponds to its altitude.
